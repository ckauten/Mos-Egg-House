Welcome to Mo's Egg House!

Mo's Egg House began with a simple dream between Rick and Mo, a husband-and-wife team who have dedicated their lives to crafting exceptional breakfast and lunch experiences. Starting their culinary journey in a challenging neighborhood in Encino, they moved through various locations, each time building a stronger and more successful establishment. From overcoming adversity—including repeated break-ins and personal health battles—to celebrating decades of community service, Rick and Mo have consistently brought their personal touch to every aspect of their restaurant. Their resilience and passion have transformed Mo's Egg House into a place where every guest is treated like family, and every meal is a reminder of home.

Our doors are open, the coffee cake is fresh, and every plate is filled with love.
Rick and Mo have built a space where community comes first, and delicious food brings us all together.
